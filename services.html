Welcome to our custom-built computer repair services page!

We offer a wide range of services to meet your needs, whether
you need a simple hardware upgrade or a full system rebuild.

Our team of experts has years of experience building and repairing
custom computers. We use the latest technology and tools to diagnose
and fix any issues you may be experiencing with your system.
Our goal is to provide you with the best possible service and get your
computer up and running as quickly as possible.

Here are some of the services we offer:

  • Custom-built computer assembly: We can help you choose the best components for your custom-built computer, assemble and test the system for you.
  • Hardware upgrades and replacements: Whether you need to replace a faulty component or want to upgrade your computer's performance, we can help you choose and install the best hardware.
  • Operating system and software installations: We can install or upgrade your operating system and software to ensure that your system is up to date and running smoothly.
  • Virus and malware removal: If your computer has been infected with a virus or malware, we can remove it and help you set up the necessary security measures to prevent future infections.
  • Data backup and recovery: If you have lost important data due to a hardware failure or other issue, we can help you recover your data and set up a backup solution to prevent future data loss.
  • System optimization and maintenance: We can help you optimize your system's performance by cleaning up unnecessary files and programs, optimizing system settings, and performing routine maintenance.

Our rates are competitive, and we offer a satisfaction guarantee on all of our services. Contact us today to schedule an appointment or to learn more about our custom-built computer repair services.
